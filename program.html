University of Michigan

The University of Michigan is one of the top schools in the country and boasts a fantastic computer science course which has you learn many languages from HTML and CSS to JavaScript and C+. With its high status as a school comes and even higher expectation for you the student. The school, according to usnews ranks 11th in Computer Science for all national univesities in both the public and private sectors. The students attending Michigan can expect top notch professors, hands on learning, and tough courses which pushes you to better understand and apply your knowledge of the programming language you are learning. Even though it is a very tough university to get in to and costs 31,000 dollars to attend, it is still a fantastic school and offers great value for the money. The computer science program at Michigan offers bachelors, masters, and PhD degrees. In order to obtain a bachelors degree at the University of Michigan, one must complete a minimum of 120 credits inclurding general elective credits.

Here are some interesting statistics about incoming freshman, graduation rates, job placement, and salary:

Entering Freshman: GPA-3.82 on a 4.0 scale, ACT-31-34, SAT-1435

Graduation Rate: 91.6%

Starting Salary: $74,400 , Median Salary: $86,900 , Top 10 Percent: $126,800+

Job Placement: 95% overall for all of Michigan
